## Goal
Propagate specific failure reasons (target drift vs ambiguous match) from _apply_autonomous_patch through to the validation diagnostics instead of collapsing into a generic error.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] When a patch fails due to ambiguous match (multiple locations), the diagnostic includes `"matched multiple locations"`.
- [ ] When a patch fails due to target drift (before-content not found), the diagnostic includes `"target drift"` or equivalent.
- [ ] When a patch fails due to low fuzzy-match confidence, the diagnostic includes the confidence score.
- [ ] Existing tests for successful patch application remain green.
- [ ] At least one new test per failure reason validates the specific diagnostic message.

## Notes
- `_apply_autonomous_patch()` in `src/higgs_agent/runtime.py:1179-1189` currently sets `reason: "target_drift_or_ambiguous"` without distinguishing the failure mode.
- `_mark_execution_failed()` at line 1436 wraps errors generically, losing the specific patch failure context.
- Consider extending the patch result dataclass with a failure-reason enum (e.g., `ambiguous_match`, `target_drift`, `low_confidence`).
- This ticket unblocks a proper fix for T-000068 by making the diagnostic messages precise.
